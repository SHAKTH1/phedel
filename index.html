OUR FOUNDER

Shivaraj Mattigatti, CEO, PHEDEL

Shivaraj Mattigatti serves as the dynamic CEO & Managing Director of Phedel Enterprises Inc, Startronix Modular Systems Pvt Ltd, and Paintel Manufacturing Inc. With a robust background spanning 17 years in the telecommunications and IT data centre industries, he has primarily focused on sales and product design. He founded Central Network Systems Inc in 2012, branching into manufacturing in Bangalore by 2014. In 2017, he acquired and merged with Startronix, ascending to the role of CEO/MD. Through his leadership, Startronix has evolved into an expansive 2,00,000 sqft state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, boasting a workforce of over 600 professionals engaged in a wide array of sectors including technology products.

ADVANCED MANUFACTURING SPACE

Extensive Fabrication With Advanced Punching and Bending Capacity

Monthly material conversion capacity reaches 3000 tons, with CNC laser cutting and advanced punching and bending machines handling various materials such as mild steel (up to 20mm thickness), stainless steel (up to 15mm), and aluminium (up to 8mm).

Robust Power Press Operations

With 10 power presses and 5 fastener insertion machines, they excel in metal shaping and forming, managing inserts up to 20mm.

Surface Finishing and Coating

Operating 5 powder coating lines with a daily capacity of 12 tons of powder, offering 1600 color options for customized finishing.

Assembly

Managing multiple assembly lines with a robust capacity for high-volume production, delivering precision and customization with a range of options tailored for original equipment manufacturers (OEMs). Our assembly services include flexible solutions to meet diverse OEM specifications, ensuring high-quality output and reliability for every product.

Wiring Solutions

Providing comprehensive wiring solutions for OEM applications, our services include the design and assembly of high-quality, customized wiring harnesses and assemblies. With a focus on precision and reliability, we support diverse OEM needs by delivering robust wiring solutions that ensure seamless integration and optimal performance in various electronic and electrical systems.

Electrical and Electronics Assembly

Specializing in advanced electrical and electronics assembly with state-of-the-art technology, our OEM-focused services offer scalable solutions for complex assemblies. We provide precise assembly capabilities with rigorous quality control, meeting the specific needs of OEM partners and ensuring optimal performance and durability of electronic components.

Plastics Expertise

Equipped with 3 injection molding machines ranging from 120 to 450 tons, they specialize in manufacturing a wide range of plastic parts and HDPE ducts.

Air Filter Manufacturing

Capable of producing 400-500 air filters daily to meet engineering and retail demands.
