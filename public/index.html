OUR FOUNDER

Shivaraj Mattigatti, CEO, PHEDEL

Shivaraj Mattigatti serves as the dynamic CEO & Managing Director of Phedel Enterprises Inc, Startronix Modular Systems Pvt Ltd, and Paintel Manufacturing Inc. With a robust background spanning 17 years in the telecommunications and IT data centre industries, he has primarily focused on sales and product design. He founded Central Network Systems Inc in 2012, branching into manufacturing in Bengaluru by 2014. In 2017, he acquired and merged with Startronix, ascending to the role of CEO/MD. Through his leadership, Startronix has evolved into an expansive 2,00,000 sqft state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, boasting a workforce of over 600 professionals engaged in a wide array of sectors including technology products.

    ADVANCED MANUFACTURING SPACE

    8 CNC Fibre Lasers

    Our facility houses 8 state-of-the-art CNC fibre lasers, ensuring precision cutting for various materials such as steel and aluminum. These lasers allow us to achieve intricate designs and shapes while maintaining the highest accuracy and efficiency across production lines.

    3 Cutting Machines

    Equipped with 3 advanced cutting machines, our plant can handle high-volume cutting operations across different metals and plastics. These machines ensure precise cuts and fast turnaround times, optimizing production efficiency.

    11 CNC Bending Machines

    Our 11 CNC bending machines are designed to deliver precise and consistent bends in metals of various thicknesses, ensuring top-quality fabrication. These machines allow for flexibility in design, offering high accuracy in complex bends for diverse industries.

    6 Special Fastener Insertion Machines

    We use 6 specialized fastener insertion machines to meet the unique fastening requirements of our clients. These machines are capable of handling various materials and provide secure, durable fastenings for intricate assembly projects.

    Spot Welding Machine

    Our advanced spot welding machine ensures strong, durable bonds between metal components. This machine excels in high-speed production environments, offering efficient welding for metal sheets of various thicknesses.

    TIG Welding Machine

    With TIG welding machines, we offer high-precision welding services for complex assemblies. This process ensures clean, accurate welds, making it ideal for projects requiring a high degree of craftsmanship and strength.

    Power Press Machine

    Our facility operates with multiple power press machines designed to shape and form metal components with precision and speed. These machines help in the efficient mass production of metal parts for various industries.

    2 Injection Moulding Machines

    We use 2 injection moulding machines to produce high-quality plastic components. With capacities ranging from 120 to 450 tons, our machines are capable of creating a wide range of plastic parts, ensuring durability and accuracy in production.

    2 Automated Pretreatment Lines

    Our 2 automated pretreatment lines prepare metal surfaces for finishing processes such as powder coating and painting. These lines ensure uniform cleaning and preparation, allowing for superior adhesion and long-lasting finishes.

    2 Converger Powder Coating Lines

    Our facility boasts 2 converger powder coating lines capable of handling large volumes of metal components. Offering over 1600 color options, our lines ensure durable, high-quality finishes tailored to specific client needs.

    In-house Electrical Wiring Harness

    Our in-house wiring harness solutions provide OEMs with high-quality, custom wiring assemblies. We ensure reliability and precision in every harness produced, supporting seamless integration into a wide range of electronic systems.

    Special Assembly Line

    We manage dedicated special assembly lines tailored for high-volume production. These lines ensure that custom OEM requirements are met with precision and efficiency, delivering high-quality assembled products to various industries.

    Advanced Machine for Packing and Loading

    Our advanced packing and loading machines streamline the process of getting products ready for distribution. These machines ensure products are securely packed and efficiently loaded, reducing handling time and ensuring safe transit.

    12 Logistic Fleets

    With a fleet of 12 logistics vehicles, we offer reliable and timely transportation for our products. Our logistics team ensures that goods are delivered efficiently, maintaining the highest standards of care and timeliness.
