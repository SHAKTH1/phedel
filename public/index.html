OUR FOUNDER

Shivaraj Mattigatti CEO, PHEDEL

Shivaraj Mattigatti serves as the dynamic CEO & Managing Director of Phedel & Startronix Modular Systems Pvt Ltd and is the proprietor of Paintel Manufacturing Inc. With a robust background spanning 17 years in the telecommunications and IT data centre industries, he has primarily focused on sales and product design. He founded Central Network Systems Inc in 2012, branching into manufacturing in Bangalore by 2014. In 2017, he acquired and merged with Startronix, ascending to the role of CEO/MD. Through his leadership, Startronix has evolved into an expansive 80,000 sqft state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, boasting a workforce of over 600 professionals engaged in a wide array of sectors including technology products

OUR INFRASTRUCTURE

Extensive Fabrication Capacity

Monthly material conversion capacity reaches 3000 tons, with CNC laser cutting machines handling various materials such as mild steel (up to 20mm thickness), stainless steel (up to 15mm), and aluminium (up to 8mm).

Robust Power Press Operations

With 10 power presses and 5 fastener insertion machines, they excel in metal shaping and forming, managing inserts up to 20mm.

Plastics Expertise

Equipped with 3 injection molding machines ranging from 120 to 450 tons, they specialize in manufacturing a wide range of plastic parts and HDPE ducts.
